# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a single-page business website for "Sai Ekvira Graphics," a commercial printing shop in Kalyan West, Mumbai, with a vibrant CMYK-inspired design, full sections showcasing services and portfolio, contact form, and English/Hindi language toggle.

**Planned changes:**
- Apply CMYK-inspired theme: deep navy (#0A1F5C) primary, orange (#FF6B00) secondary, cyan/magenta/yellow accents, white backgrounds, bold sans-serif typography, fully mobile-responsive
- Sticky navbar with logo/business name, smooth-scroll links (About, Services, Gallery, Why Us, Contact), transparent-to-solid-navy on scroll, hamburger menu on mobile, and English/Hindi toggle button
- Full-width Hero section with printing-themed background image, overlay with business name and tagline, three CTA buttons: Call Now (tel:09702477181), WhatsApp Us (https://wa.me/919702477181), Get Quote (scrolls to contact)
- About Us section with business description, operating hours (Mon–Sat 10AM–9PM, Sunday Closed), and full address
- Our Services section with 8 responsive grid cards (icon, name, short description) with hover accent effects: Flex Banner Printing, Digital Printing, Visiting Cards, Brochures & Pamphlets, Poster Printing, Signage & Shop Boards, Vehicle Graphics & Branding, Photo Prints & Wedding Cards
- Gallery/Portfolio section with 8–10 sample images in a responsive grid, with lightbox/modal on click
- Why Choose Us section with 6 icon-accompanied feature cards
- Contact section with React-managed form (Name, Phone, Service Type dropdown, Message), success message on submit, clickable phone link, embedded Google Maps iframe (Adharwadi, Kalyan West), and fixed bottom-right floating WhatsApp button
- Footer with business name, address, clickable phone, Instagram & Facebook icon links, copyright line
- English/Hindi toggle: switches section headings, nav labels, and key button labels to Hindi equivalents

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors can browse a professional, vibrant single-page website for Sai Ekvira Graphics, view services and portfolio, contact the business via form or direct WhatsApp/call links, and toggle the interface between English and Hindi.
